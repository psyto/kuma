The Problem with Basis Trade Vaults

Fixed leverage (3-5x) — Reckless during volatility spikes. Health ratio deteriorates rapidly in a short squeeze.
No cost awareness — Frequent rotation eats more in trading fees than the funding harvested.
Slow reaction — Hourly scans, 4-hour rebalances. By the time the bot reacts, the damage is done.
No bear market plan — When funding turns negative, the vault bleeds.

Kuma's Approach

Dynamic leverage — 2x in calm markets → 0x in extreme vol. Scales inversely with risk.
Cost gate — Only enters markets where expected funding exceeds round-trip trading costs.
30-second health monitoring — Catches margin deterioration 240x faster than hourly checks.
30% lending floor — Base yield even when all funding is negative.
